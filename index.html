YACoin is Your Alternative Currency with an advanced economic model.

About

YACoin was created in May of 2013 and was the first to introduce the N parameter of Scrypt. Sticking to its roots, YAC will maintain the inventive scrypt-chacha (aka scrypt-jane). NFactor will be set at 21 to be ASIC resistance but also mineable for many CPUs and GPUs.

Limited Inflation Rate

Maxed at ~2% per year, inflation rate provides a sophisticated system to account for economic growth.

Atomic swap support

OP codes for atomic swap/loans capabilities implemented.

Timelocked Stakeholder Accounts

Hash Time-Locked holdings by early stakeholders provides extra assurance to new investors of long-term buy in to the project.
